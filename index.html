Story

In the mythical land of Westeros, noble houses vie for the Iron Throne. The honorable Stark family faces betrayal as Eddard Stark uncovers a dark secret. Meanwhile, Daenerys Targaryen, exiled princess, hatches three dragon eggs and aims to reclaim her birthright.

The cunning Lannisters play a dangerous political game, with Tyrion Lannister navigating the treacherous waters of King's Landing. Beyond the Wall, Jon Snow joins the Night's Watch and discovers the looming threat of the White Walkers.

As war engulfs Westeros, Robb Stark leads a rebellion against the tyranny of King Joffrey Baratheon. The Red Wedding brings a tragic turn, altering the course of the North forever. Arya Stark embarks on a quest for vengeance, training with the Faceless Men. Bran Stark discovers his mystical abilities and the impending danger from the Night King. Cersei Lannister's thirst for power culminates in her coronation as Queen. Eddard's illegitimate son, Jon Snow, becomes the Lord Commander of the Night's Watch, facing betrayal and resurrection.

The Battle of the Bastards sees Jon reclaim Winterfell, but the threat of the Night King intensifies. Daenerys finally sails to Westeros, forming alliances to challenge Cersei's rule. The Night King breaches the Wall with his undead army, setting the stage for the ultimate battle between the living and the dead.
